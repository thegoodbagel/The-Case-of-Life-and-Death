Mother or child?

Abortion is one of the most controversial and hotly debated topics of the twentieth century. The main argument revolves around whether life begins at conception, but safety, autonomy, religion and science are also significant points. Those who believe abortion is a right identify themselves as pro-choice, while those who oppose abortion are pro-life.
